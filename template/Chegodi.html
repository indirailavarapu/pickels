Chegodi

Chegodi is one of the healthiest dishes from South Indian cuisine.
It is a perfect choice for a wholesome and filling breakfast meal,
when paired with a bowl of sambar and chutney.
These are delicious and protein-packed rice and lentil crepes,
especially popular in Tamil Nadu, South India.
