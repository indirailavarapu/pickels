magaya Ingredients

Raw Mango:Tangy, fibrous, and vitamin-rich. Red Chilies:Spicy, vibrant, and heat-packed. Mustard Seeds:Adds crunch, aroma, and depth. Fenugreek:Bitter, aromatic, and aids digestion. Oil:Enhances flavor and preserves well.

magaya pachadi.

Magaya pachadi is a traditional, spicy, and tangy Telugu-style mango pickle made with sun-dried raw mangoes and spices. It is a favorite accompaniment to hot rice and ghee in Telugu cuisine.
