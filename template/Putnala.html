Putnala Chutney Ingredients

Rice: Staple grain choice
Roasted Gram: Base for chutney
Red Chilies: Adds spicy flavor.
Garlic: Enhances taste and aroma.
Salt: Seasons the mixture well.
Blend: Grind all into fine powder.

Putnala Chutney.

Putnala Chutney is one of the healthiest dishes from the South Indian cuisine
Thus, Putnala Chutney is the perfect choice for a wholesome
and filling breakfast meal, when paired with a bowl of sambar
and chutney as well. Basically, these are delicious and protein-packed rice and lentil crepes,
which are especially popular in the cuisine of Tamil Nadu in South India.
