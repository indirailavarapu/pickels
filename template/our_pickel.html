Sourced with care

We use 100% Indian Pickel. Grown
above 5300ft in the southern part of
India, and sourced directly from
farms,our plantation pickel is some
of the best around.

An exact roast.

Every pickel reacts differently to
roasting. The right roast enhances the
flavours we want, while keeping
bitterness away. We spent months
obsessively testing our pickel to fine-
tune our signature taste. Today, we
roast in small batches to maintain
that same high standard throughout.

SM Foods

SM Foods was established in 2025 with a motive to manufacture authentic South Indian pickles. All these wholesome products provide an enticing blend of taste, and are made from fresh, high-quality ingredients procured directly from the farmer. All our products are nourishing as well as tasty and 100% natural.
