Challa Mirapakaya.

Challa Mirapakaya is a traditional Telangana delicacy made by soaking green chilies in buttermilk and drying them under the sun. These sun-dried chilies are later deep-fried until crisp and served as a spicy side dish with curd rice or dal rice. The unique fermentation process gives them a tangy and mildly spicy flavor, making them a favorite in Telugu households.
