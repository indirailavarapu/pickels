Adai.

Adai is one of the healthiest dishes from the South Indian cuisine
Thus, Adai Dosa is the perfect choice for a wholesome
and filling breakfast meal, when paired with a bowl of sambar
and chutney as well. Basically, these are delicious and protein-packed rice and lentil crepes,
which are especially popular in the cuisine of Tamil Nadu in South India.
