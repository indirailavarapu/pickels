Nuvvula Podi Ingredients.

White sesame seeds (Nuvvulu):1 cup Dried red chilies:5 to 6 (adjust spice level as needed) Cumin seeds (Jeera):1 tsp Garlic cloves:3 to 4 (optional) Tamarind:1 small piece (optional, adds slight tanginess)

Nuvvula Podi.

Nuvvula Podi is a flavorful and aromatic spice powder made with roasted sesame seeds and spices. It is a staple in many Telugu households and is known for its nutty, slightly sweet, and spicy taste. This podi pairs perfectly with hot rice and ghee or can be used as a side dish for idli and dosa.
