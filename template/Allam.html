Spices :Adds heat, aroma, and depth.

Allam pachadi.

Allam pachadi is a popular Telugu style ginger
pickle made with fresh ginger, spices, tamarind
& jaggery. It is most commonly eaten during
winters or monsoon with rice, idli, dosa,
pesarattu and ghee. Ginger pickle is so very
popular in Telugu cuisine that needs no
introduction. Allam translates to ginger
and pachadi is a pickle. Hence called allam
pachadi in Telugu.
