Kandi Podi Ingredients.

Toor dal (Pigeon pea dal):1 cup Urad dal (Black gram dal):2 tbsp Dried red chilies:6 to 8 (adjust spice level as needed) Cumin seeds (Jeera):1 tsp Black pepper:1 tsp Garlic cloves:3 to 4

Kandi Podi.

Kandi Podi is a traditional Telugu-style lentil powder made with roasted toor dal (pigeon pea) and spices. It is packed with protein and is a flavorful accompaniment to hot rice mixed with ghee. This podi is widely enjoyed in Telugu households for its nutty, spicy, and aromatic taste.
