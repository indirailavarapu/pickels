Avakaya Ingredients

Raw mangoes:1 kg (firm and sour mangoes) Mustard powder: 200 g Red chili powder: 200 g (preferably Kashmiri or Guntur chili) Salt:150 g (rock salt preferred) Fenugreek seeds (Methi):2 tbsp (slightly roasted and powdered) Turmeric powder:1 tbsp

Avakaya pachadi.

Avakaya is a traditional and popular South Indian pickle, especially famous in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is a spicy and tangy pickle made with raw mangoes, mustard powder, and a blend of aromatic spices. This pickle is known for its bold flavors and is commonly enjoyed with rice and ghee.
