Nallakaram Podi Ingredients

Urad dal (Black gram dal): ½ cup Chana dal (Bengal gram dal):¼ cup Dried red chilies:8 to 10 (adjust spice level as needed) Coriander seeds:2 tbsp Cumin seeds (Jeera):1 tsp

Nallakaram Podi.

Nallakaram Podi is a traditional Telugu spice powder known for its bold, spicy, and slightly tangy flavors. It is made with roasted dals, dried red chilies, and garlic, making it a perfect accompaniment for idli, dosa, or hot rice mixed with ghee. This podi is a staple in many Telugu households and enhances the taste of any meal.
